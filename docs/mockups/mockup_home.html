Landslide at KM67 — Quản Bạ → Yên Minh

Posted 13 Jun 2026 · Hà Giang Traffic Department

A significant landslide has partially blocked National Highway 4C at KM67 on the route between Quản Bạ and Yên Minh. Local authorities are clearing debris and traffic is being directed through a single alternating lane.

What to expect

Expect delays of 30–45 minutes during peak hours. Larger vehicles may be held until the carriageway is fully reopened. Conditions can change quickly after rain, so check before you set off.

Recommended detour

Riders heading to Đồng Văn can take the QL4C → DT181 connector via Lũng Tám and rejoin the loop near Yên Minh. Add roughly 25 minutes to your journey.
