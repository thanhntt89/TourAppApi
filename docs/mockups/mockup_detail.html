French Bunker Ruins

Núi Cấm • 0.8 km away

The French bunker ruins on Núi Cấm are concrete structures located on the prominent hill directly overlooking the center of Hà Giang city. They exist as a functional stopping point along the uphill walking tracks...

Rather than a separate, cordoned-off historical monument, these remains are a natural extension of a regular walk up the mountain. From up here on the ridge, the entire layout of Hà Giang becomes much easier to understand.
